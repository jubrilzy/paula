Meet Paula Wood
My Story

From a small idea to a
global consultancy.

I founded Exam Officers & Data Solutions Ltd after many years working in senior roles within education, training and consultancy. What began as a small idea to support centres with exams, access arrangements and data quickly grew into a respected global training and consultancy company.

We are trusted for our professionalism, high-impact delivery with a calm and clear approach.

Over the last 15 plus years, the company has supported thousands of professionals through training, mentoring, strategy development and operational guidance. My work has taken me across the UK and internationally, giving me a unique understanding of how people learn, lead and grow in fast-moving environments.

"Behind every strong business or successful career is someone who found clarity about what they want, why they want it, and how to make it happen."

And while the company has evolved, one thing has never changed: my belief that clarity builds confidence and confidence changes everything.
