One to One Career Coaching

Find clarity in your career and confidence in your next step.

Over the past 20 years, I have supported people at every stage of their professional journey - those stepping into leadership roles, those returning after a break, those transitioning into new industries, and those simply wanting to reconnect with what feels meaningful, motivating and achievable.

Together, we will focus on what is important to you and create a plan that feels realistic.

"You deserve a career that supports your life - not one that drains it."

Through our sessions, you can:

  • Gain clarity on your career goals and direction
  • Rebuild confidence after setbacks or change
  • Identify your strengths and how to use them effectively
  • Learn how to present yourself powerfully in applications and interviews
  • Create a clear, practical action plan to move forward
